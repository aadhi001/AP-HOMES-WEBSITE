About us

AP HOMES was founded in 2012 by Mr.Pandian Ramalingam. Before starting the company, Mr.Pandian worked in Singapore on various civil engineering companies for sixteen years. He got his bachelor degree in Civil Engineering from Kalasalingam College of Engineering. He has 30 years of experience in the field. AP Homes since its inductance has been the go-to choice for many home buyers. The company is best known for using quality materials for construction and for offering competitive prices.
